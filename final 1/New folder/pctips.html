PC Troubleshooting Tips!


  • Computer won't boot up? Start your computer, as it is booting up, hit F8 to get the boot options menu. Select Repair Your Computer from the list.

  • Computer has numerous errors, viruses, missing files, or won't boot properly? Try a system restore. Start button/windows button, search for "system restore" or navigate to All Programs, Accessories, System Tools, System Restore and follow the directions.

  • Slow computer? A full hard drive "Local Disk (C:)" will slow down your computer. Free up some space by deleting apps and media you don't use.

  • Internet slow? Delete your history, cookies and cache. Make sure your network card's drives are up to date, and try restarting your modem or router!

  • Computer keeps restarting? First make sure windows is updated and run a virus and malware scan.

  • Constant "Security" warnings? Check to make sure that your computers clock shows the correct date and time. If not, right-click and set it manually.

  • Can't open a file? Check the file extension (jpeg, gif, png, doc, etc.) and use Google to find the right software program to open it.
